What's Care Today For Tomorrow?

As the team of “Care Today for Tomorrow”, we aspire to contribute to the social and mental well-being of vulnerable children in shelters by hosting a variety of interactive sessions that will improve their social skills and create a positive emotional environment.

Project Summary

Considering the necessity of care for the kids in shelter, our project is aiming to contribute to their development and interaction. This project is approaching the problem from different perspectives via negotiating with different colleagues and professionals in this field. We will have collaboration with our partners till the end of this project who will help us throughout the project.
